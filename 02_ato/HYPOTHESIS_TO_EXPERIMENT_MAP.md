# Hypothesis to Experiment Map

Purpose: convert claims into examinable evidence chains.

Template:

Hypothesis
Unknown
Experiment
Result
Evidence
Cost linkage

Examples:

Multi-agent orchestration
Unknown: can agents coordinate deterministically?
Experiment: bridge + orchestration runs
Result: partial
Evidence: commits, runtime logs, conversations
Cost linkage: cloud, development, labour

AI cognitive diversity
Unknown: optimal AI involvement by cohort
Experiment: longitudinal testing
Result: partial
Evidence: research datasets and analysis
Cost linkage: research labour and tools
