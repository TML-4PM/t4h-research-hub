## The discoveries that matter most

### 1. ResearchOS exists in pieces

The system is not just a research folder. It links research, product, finance, IP, evidence, websites, runtime code, deployments and proof doctrine.

### 2. AI Sweet Spots is not the parent of everything

AI Sweet Spots is a major calibration layer. It should not absorb DRA, ConsentX, GC-BAT, Digital Child Protection, Rhythm Method, CalmBound, Doolittles, Reality Ledger Evidence Science or MAAT.

### 3. The 47-direction backlog was located

The AI Sweet Spots Novel Ideas Register appears to be the real 47-direction source. It includes 8 domains, novel research directions, and major IP/trademark/patent claims. It needs parsing into a structured backlog.

### 4. RDTI generation infrastructure is real

The `mcp-command-centre/scripts/rdti/README.md` evidence is highly material. It states the RDTI substantiation pack has 134 total docs, 133 complete, W0-W10 wave coverage, generation scripts, source tables, and Command Centre query keys.

This proves a generated evidence pack infrastructure exists. It does not prove every underlying artefact file is independently present or complete.

### 5. MAAT is not just finance

MAAT appears to be the bridge from transaction -> R&D project -> research study -> IP asset -> grant -> evidence -> generated pack.

### 6. IP counts conflict

These may represent different scopes or snapshots, but the conflict is real. Do not use any single count externally until reconciled.

### 13. Doolittles has commercial packaging

`Doolittles Sellable Service-Pack Subset - Canonical Source v0.1` means Doolittles is not only a SpeechEvent theory. It has a service-pack/commercial spine candidate.

### 14. Digital Child Protection, DRA, ConsentX, GC-BAT, Rhythm Method and CalmBound are not minor notes

Each surfaced as asset-backed or repo-backed enough to remain separate streams.
