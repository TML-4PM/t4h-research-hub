## Nuances and variances that matter

### Generated evidence versus source evidence

The generation infrastructure is powerful, but generated documents may look stronger than the source store proves.

A generated evidence document is evidence that a system generated a claim pack. It is not automatically proof that the underlying source artefact exists, is complete, is signed, or is admissible.

Vercel deployment confirms a project is deployed, but not necessarily that it is the correct product, using correct data, wired to correct domains, or supported by current source-of-truth records.

Similarly, repo existence does not equal implementation proof.
