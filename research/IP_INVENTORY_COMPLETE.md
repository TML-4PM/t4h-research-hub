### 1.1 AI Sweet Spots Research ⭐ CRITICAL
**Priority:** Academic publication + patent claims  
**Status:** Dataset complete (4,247 participants), paper draft ready

**Core Innovation:**  
Largest neurodivergent AI productivity study globally. Demonstrates differential cognitive effects of AI across neurotypes with unprecedented effect sizes.

**Key Metrics:**
- 4,247 total participants (1,267 neurodivergent, 2,980 neurotypical)
- 6-month longitudinal cohort across 9 Australian organizations
- ADHD: 44% productivity gain (Cohen's d = 1.81)
- Neurotypical: 3% productivity gain (Cohen's d = 0.19)
- **12x differential in outcomes**

**Optimal AI Intensity ("Sweet Spots"):**

- Dyslexic: 45% AI involvement (38% gains, d = 1.01)
- AuDHD: 41% gains (d = 1.45)
- Neurotypical: 25% AI involvement (diminishing returns above)

**EEG Subsample (N=423):**
- ADHD at optimal: 58% reduction in prefrontal executive load
- Neurotypical at heavy AI: 23% neural disengagement

**IP Opportunities:**

3. **Academic Paper:** "AI Sweet Spots: Differential Productivity Effects Across Cognitive Profiles"

**Market Validation:**
