Eight ways we can work together

Format
Menu

The Future Frontiers content fits inside a lot of different shapes. Single segment, recurring slot, mini-series, panel, deep-dive doc, live event, audio spin-off, or just background research. Pick one — or stack several.

8 formats
2 producer-recommended
Stackable — most combine

Most pitches assume one format. This is a menu. The recommended pick depends on slot, audience and how much time the show wants to commit. Two formats are flagged as producer recommended for the strongest combination of impact and sustainability — but every option here is real.

F1

Single Segment

One topic, one conversation

Best forIntroducing a frontier theme to a general audience
Length7–10 min
Sample structure
00:00Eyebrow hook — the unexpected fact
01:00Why it matters now
02:30The human story (case / patient / worker)
05:00The big question
07:00What's at stake if we get it wrong
Deliverables Pre-brief, talking points doc, post-segment recap
F2

Recurring Expert Slot

Same chair, every week or fortnight

Best forBuilding a trusted voice the audience returns to
Length5–8 min
Sample structure
WeeklyOne frontier story currently moving
MonthlyBigger thematic piece
Ad-hocReactive — when a breakthrough or scandal lands
Deliverables Weekly briefing doc, shared editorial calendar
F3

Mini-Series (5–8 episodes)

One spine, told properly

Best forA coherent arc viewers can binge or follow weekly
Length10–15 min per episode
Sample structure
Ep 1The premise — why this, why now
Ep 2–6One frontier per episode, building
Ep 7The convergence — what happens when they combine
Ep 8The decision — what we choose next
Deliverables Full series treatment, episode briefs, supplementary research pack
F4

Panel Discussion

Multiple voices, real disagreement

Best forTopics where the right answer is genuinely contested
Length15–25 min
Sample structure
OpenFrame the question, introduce panel
Block 1Each panelist's strongest case
Block 2Cross-examination / disagreement
CloseWhere consensus exists, where it doesn't
Deliverables Panelist suggestions across academia / industry / government, briefing material per voice
F5

Deep-Dive Documentary

One story, fully unpacked

Best forA standout single piece on a high-stakes frontier
Length30–60 min
Sample structure
Act 1Origin — who saw it first, what they saw
Act 2The hard middle — failures, false starts, ethical pressure
Act 3The moment — what changed, what's next
Deliverables Research pack, interview shortlist, on-camera availability, archival sourcing
F6

Live Q&A or Debate

Audience in the room

Best forEvents, conferences, partner activations, town halls
Length45–60 min
Sample structure
OpenProvocation — one strong claim, defended
MiddleAudience challenge / counter-arguments
CloseWhat changed in the room
Deliverables Pre-event run-of-show, audience question seeding, post-event clips
F7

Audio / Podcast Spin-Off

Longer-form, off-screen

Best forExtending the reach beyond broadcast
Length30–60 min
Sample structure
CompanionDirector's-cut version of the TV segment
StandaloneLonger conversations than time allows on-air
MailbagAudience questions answered with depth
Deliverables Episode structure, guest curation, ongoing content calendar
F8

Editorial / Background Research

Behind-the-scenes contribution

Best forStrengthening other segments — even without on-air presence
LengthProject-based
Sample structure
BriefsPer-segment research dossiers
SourcesVerified expert contacts and primary references
Fact-checkPre-broadcast review on technical claims
Deliverables Whatever a senior producer needs — confidential, fast, well-sourced

Most of these stack.

A working combination: a recurring weekly expert slot anchors the audience. A mini-series runs in parallel for higher production episodes. A podcast spin-off gives the long-form audience somewhere to go. And background research keeps every other segment on the show sharper. We can scope to whatever budget and slot exists.

Anchor Recurring weekly slot
Arc Mini-series running in parallel
Depth Podcast / audio spin-off
Spine Background research across the show
